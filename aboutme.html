MYSELF


Nur Arisya Adina is how I go by. Growing up, I was my family's second kid. Among my mother's children,
I was the closest. Together with my sister, I have a brother. Kedah is where I was raised and where
I was born. Being an introvert with a shy personality, I tend to be quiet around strangers but enjoy
being with close friends and family. Few of the people closest to me helped me succeed in whatever I
set out to do, even though they weren't always there to support me. Sports really interest me. Netball
is the sport of interest, but I enjoy watching a wide variety of sports on television. I participated
in nearly every athletic event the school hosted when I was in school.
